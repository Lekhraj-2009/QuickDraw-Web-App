QuickDraw!

Starting the Game

  • Before starting the game, read all the given instructions carefully.

  • Click the 'START' Button below to start the game.

How to Play?

  • You will be given the names of different real-life objects (for e.g., alarm clock, banana, windmill, etc.)

  • You have 15 seconds to draw the object on the board.

  • Your score and the time left will be displayed on the screen.

  • If the drawn object is recognised, you get a point. Else, no points will be added to your score.

  • If the timer strikes 0, your drawing will be cleared and you will get no points.

Ending the Game

  • To end the game, click the 'END GAME' Button on the top-left of the screen. You will be redirected to the home page.
